Data Policy

This document's purpose is to describe what kind of data we gather and how we handle it. It is important to mention that we do not try to gather any sensitive information or private content of any kind.


  • The Service does not directly hold any data regarding its Users. Any contact information that might be stored comes solely from the content published on the crawled websites.

  • The Service's clear scope is to index and make searchable data and content that is already public and accessible on a website. All the data published on the crawled website will be indexed and made searchable via a REST endpoint. If you do not want some content to be searchable, then it should not be published on the crawled website.

  • No index action is ever triggered without the User's command. No action is scheduled or repeated even if it fails.

  • All the crawled content is sent to Elasticsearch in the AWS cloud. We do not store in any other way. The Content is not altered in any way, it is fetched and indexed as-is.

  • We do not sell your data to anyone, although it might be accessible to anyone through the search endpoint (again, this data is supposed to be public anyway, on your website!)
